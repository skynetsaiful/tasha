I want to create products that reach out and empower every kind of person. Like our minds, design has no limitations, only solutions for now and for the future.

Hi, I'm Tasha!

I’m a UX Designer who never gets bored with resolving multi-faceted problems and loves to influence others through technology and design. I am also a Sociology major at Texas Woman’s University, a cool mom, and an amazing wife. In a nutshell, I’m Superhuman.


I am the product of artists and thinkers. Growing up, I was always drawing, painting, and trying to figure out how my art could make someone else happy. I dabbled in photoshop and altered images, or created my own magazines, wrote poetry and always found myself navigating others through their problems, and some form of art was always at the end my guidance. I’ve always been creative and inquisitive, and these qualities followed me throughout life and lent themselves to my strong work ethic and passion for helping others.

My design philosophy

Research

I love research, and I believe that the best research is the kind that makes us reach for more. Understanding people, their behaviors, their decision process and what motivates them, is the only way we can design effictively.

Creative Manifestation

As a designer, I start with pure intention of creating something for someone else.The act of going through several processes to bring my vision into a physical state is intensely meaningful and a social responsibility.

User Empowerment

Designers are responsible for encouraging and empower every kind of user to use more digital products to make their lives easier, experiences more pleasant.

When I'm not working.

I’m spending time researching random things and being loved and inspired by my beautiful family.
